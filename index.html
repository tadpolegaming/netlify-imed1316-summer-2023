Twitching Clown

This twitching clown twists at the waist and shakes manically at unsuspecting guests. He terrifies as he moves to classical music, moving his jaw up and down with light-up eyes and laughing manically. He's here to provide nightmares to all your guests for years to come!

Pesky the Clown

Don’t turn your back on 7-foot Pesky the Clown, he just might go crazy! That’s not paint on his costume. Watch him joke for you as his eyes glow yellow, head turns side to side and jaw moves. His classic clown face makes for a good scare this Halloween, just make sure you give him your undivided attention or else.

Jumping Clown

This horrific clown decoration springs forward to greet you as he chuckles mirthlessly and his unearthly LED eyes glow. Terrifying carnival music plays as he jumps up and then settles back to wait for the next victim. Bring him to life by simply using the step-here pad or the infrared sensor.

Rotting Reaper

Khronos, the Rotting Reaper, will terrify your trick-or-treaters with his evil towering presence, tall scythe, plus glowing red LED eyes, chest, and skull prop. Bone-chilling animation includes Khronos moving his mouth as he speaks 3 frightening phrases while turning his body from side to side.

Lunging Reaper

This exclusive, life-sized Lunging Reaper Animatronic Decoration is swathed in dark, tattered robes tied at the waist with a long cord. Skeletal hands poke out from the sleeves, and one holds a realistic scythe. A skeletal face with glowing eyes peers from beneath the hood. Watch out, because it will try to snatch you when it's activated!

Servo Witch

This Crone Witch takes on the night this season warning you about all the spooky happenings this Halloween. Servo motors give her life-like movement. If you have a trick or treater in need for some tips tonight, send them to this Servo Witch. They'll be able to spot her from across the room with her glowing LED eyes.

Fortune Teller Witch

Alma, the Fortune Teller Witch, will intrigue any trick-or-treaters who dare knock on your door with her color-changing magic ball and glowing white eyes. Mara, the Macabre Witch, is an evil ancient soul of the underworld allowed to roam freely upon the earth to terrorize the living. Careful, you never know when or where she'll turn up next.

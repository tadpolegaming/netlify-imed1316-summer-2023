Home Consultations


Transform your home into a spectacular Halloween wonderland with our professional decoration consultation services! Our expert decorators will work closely with you to bring your spooky visions to life, creating an atmosphere that will impress all your trick-or-treaters and guests.

We're very busy this time of year, so please refer to our rather inconsistent schedule. Due to high demand, consultation appointments may book quickly. We recommend scheduling at least one week in advance to secure your desired time slot.


    Considerations

  • Space Assessment

    • Evaluating the available indoor and outdoor spaces for decoration placement, ensuring they accommodate the desired themed displays without overcrowding or obstructing pathways.

  • Power Access

    • Identifying accessible power sources for lighting, animatronics, and other electrical decorations to ensure safety and practicality in setup.
